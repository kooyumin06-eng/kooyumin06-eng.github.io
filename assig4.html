Assignment 4 - Newsletter of Johor Port Berhad

Link: Assignment 4

Reflection:

For assignment 4, we visited Johor Port Berhad in Pasir Gudang. From this industrial visit, I gained an understanding of how IT systems manage large-scale data flows and the importance of maintaining a high level of network security. The visit was an eye-opening experience for me as I had the opportunity to visit one of Malaysia’s ports. Moreover, I realized that some of the staff were graduates from our university inspired me to study hard so that have the opportunity to join such as organization in the future. For improvement, I understand that the tour around the port was conducted from inside the bus for safety reasons. However, if possible, I hope to have a closer visit in safe areas in the future to better observe the port’s operations.
