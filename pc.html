PC Assemble - Lab and Quiz

Reflection:

For PC assemble, we learned the steps to assemble and reassemble a PC. I was really happy and grateful to have the opportunity to gain hands-on experience in putting a PC together. It was my first time seeing all the internal components of a PC which helped me understand how each part works. After the lab, we had a quiz. It reinforced my understanding and helped me remember the steps more clearly. This experience improved both my practical skills and my confidence in working with computer hardware.
